Getting Started

Essential information for new users of the OneVictoria platform

OneVictoria is the official resident engagement platform for the Municipality of Victoria. This digital hub connects citizens with municipal services, community events, volunteer programs, and civic announcements. The platform enables residents to:

  • Register for community events and programs
  • Submit volunteer applications for municipal initiatives
  • Track community service hour contributions
  • Make monetary or in-kind donations to support local causes
  • Receive real-time notifications regarding community updates
  • Manage personal profile information securely

Account registration is a straightforward process:

  1. Navigate to the OneVictoria portal homepage
  2. On the authentication panel, select "Create Account"
  3. Complete the registration form with the following required fields:
    • Full legal name
    • Valid email address
    • Contact phone number
    • Age (must be 15 years or older)
    • Gender
    • Complete residential address
  4. Create a secure password (minimum 6 characters)
  5. Confirm your password by re-entering it
  6. Click "Create Account" to finalize registration

Recommendation: Use an active email address as this will serve as your primary credential for account recovery and notifications.

To access your account:

  1. Visit the OneVictoria portal
  2. On the sign-in panel, enter your registered email address
  3. Enter your account password
  4. Select "Access Portal"
  5. Upon successful authentication, you will be directed to your personalized dashboard

Important: If you are unable to access your account due to forgotten credentials, please refer to the Troubleshooting section or contact our support team.

Account Management

Managing your profile, credentials, and account settings

  1. Navigate to the Profile section via the sidebar
  2. Select the "Edit Profile" button to enable editing mode
  3. Modify the editable fields: Mobile Number, Address, and Profile Picture
  4. Select "Save Changes" to apply your updates

Note: Legal name, email address, gender, and age are immutable fields. To request changes to these fields, please contact our support department.

Events & Registration

Discovering, registering for, and managing community events

  1. Navigate to the Events tab from the sidebar navigation
  2. Browse the grid of available upcoming events
  3. Click the "Register" button on any event card of interest
  4. A confirmation notification will appear upon successful registration
  5. The registered event will be visible in your My Events section

Yes, registration cancellation is available:

  1. Go to the My Events section
  2. Locate the event you wish to cancel
  3. Click the "Cancel" button on the respective event card
  4. Confirm the cancellation in the dialog prompt

Recommendation: Please cancel at least 24 hours prior to the event to allow other residents the opportunity to participate.

  • Registered Your registration has been successfully processed.
  • Completed You attended the event and received service hour credit.
  • Cancelled The registration has been cancelled either by you or the event organizer.

Volunteering

Applying as a volunteer and contributing to municipal programs

  1. Access the Volunteer tab from the sidebar
  2. Your name will be automatically populated from your account profile
  3. Select your Primary Skill from the comprehensive dropdown menu
  4. Upload a Skill Verification document (certificate, credential, or proof of expertise)
  5. Indicate your Experience Level and Availability
  6. Include any supplementary notes regarding your experience or qualifications
  7. Click "Submit Application" to finalize

Applications undergo review by municipal organizers. You will receive a notification upon status change.

The platform accepts the following file formats (maximum file size: 5MB):

  • PDF — Certificates, training completion documents, professional licenses
  • JPG / PNG — Photographs of certificates, identification cards, or credentials
  • DOC / DOCX — Reference letters, experience verification documents

Service Hours

Tracking community service contributions and certifications

Service hours are accrued through active participation in registered community events. When you attend an event for which you have registered, the event organizer may certify your participation hours. These certified hours are recorded and displayed in your Service Hours tab, contributing to your community service record.

  • Certified Hours have been verified and approved by an authorized event organizer.
  • Pending Hours are awaiting verification from the event organizer.

Donations

Making contributions to support community initiatives

  1. Navigate to the Donations tab
  2. Complete the donation form by specifying the item or monetary amount and the intended purpose
  3. Click "Submit Donation"
  4. A payment modal will appear where you may select your preferred payment method
  5. Choose from the available options: GCash, PayMaya, Bank Transfer, or Cash
  6. Enter the donation amount and your full name
  7. Click "Pay Now" to complete the transaction

The platform currently supports the following payment channels:

  • GCash — Mobile wallet payment processing
  • PayMaya — Digital wallet transaction service
  • Bank Transfer — Direct deposit to municipal accounts
  • Cash — In-person cash remittance at designated offices

Notifications

Managing real-time alerts and system notifications

Notifications can be accessed through two methods:

  • Quick Preview: Click the bell icon located in the sidebar header to display a dropdown of recent notifications
  • Comprehensive View: Navigate to the Notifications tab for a complete, chronological listing of all notifications

There are two methods to mark notifications as read:

  • Select "Mark All Read" from either the notification dropdown or the full notification list
  • Click on an individual notification to view its details, which automatically marks it as read

Troubleshooting

Resolving common technical issues and account problems

Password recovery is facilitated through our support team:

  1. Contact our support department using one of the methods listed in the Contact Support section below
  2. Provide your registered email address for identity verification
  3. A support representative will guide you through the password reset procedure

Please attempt the following troubleshooting steps:

  • Refresh your browser window (F5 or Ctrl+R)
  • Clear your browser cache and stored cookies
  • Attempt to access the platform using an alternative browser (Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, or Microsoft Edge recommended)
  • Verify that your internet connection is stable
  • Ensure that JavaScript is enabled in your browser settings

Volunteer applications undergo manual review by municipal event organizers. The standard processing period is 1–3 business days. You will receive a notification when your application status changes. If more than three business days have elapsed without an update, please contact our support team for assistance.
